About Me

Hi, my name is Sameer! I'm an AI Engineer at IBM, where I lead projects focused on generative AI and large language models for enterprise solutions. My work involves designing and implementing cutting-edge AI-driven tools and solutions to solve complex business challenges. Prior to IBM, I gained valuable experience at UCSF, where I worked on applying AI in healthcare, and at Salesforce, where I focused on building ML solutions to tackle customer success and customer churn.

I hold a BS in Engineering Mathematics and Statistics from UC Berkeley, which laid the foundation for my analytical and problem-solving approach to AI. In addition to my professional work, I'm currently building a side project, the Fitness AI App, aimed at personalizing fitness and health insights using AI. I’m passionate about blending my technical skills with real-world applications, and I’m always excited to explore new challenges in AI and beyond.
